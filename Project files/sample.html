Understanding Poultry Health

Poultry farming is a cornerstone of food supply and nutrition in many communities. However, disease outbreaks in poultry can cause immense economic losses and threaten food security. Some common diseases include Coccidiosis, Avian Influenza, and Newcastle Disease, which can severely affect bird health and productivity.

At PoultryDetect, we aim to revolutionize disease detection using artificial intelligence. Our platform allows users to upload poultry images and receive predictions about potential infections using deep learning image classification techniques.

Coccidiosis is caused by a protozoan parasite and typically leads to diarrhea, reduced feed intake, and stunted growth. Early detection is critical for minimizing spread across flocks.

Avian Influenza (Bird Flu) is a highly contagious viral disease that impacts both small-scale and commercial poultry operations. Symptoms may include respiratory distress, swelling, and sudden death.

Newcastle Disease affects the respiratory and nervous systems and can lead to tremors, twisted necks, and paralysis in severe cases.

Our system is trained on hundreds of images and fine-tuned through transfer learning using models like ResNet. This enhances its ability to differentiate between subtle visual symptoms and normal bird physiology.

How it Works:
1. Upload a clear image of the poultry.
2. The model processes the image through a deep CNN.
3. Results are provided instantly with a confidence level.
4. Users are advised on next steps like isolation, medication, or contacting a vet.

Our goal is to assist farmers in remote and underserved regions who may not have immediate access to veterinary care. By using mobile phones and uploading images, they can get instant insights and save entire flocks.

Tips for Healthy Poultry:
- Maintain clean and dry housing.
- Ensure access to clean water and quality feed.
- Follow proper vaccination schedules.
- Regularly monitor for symptoms.
- Use biosecurity measures to prevent disease spread.

PoultryDetect is here to empower the farming community with technology-driven solutions, enhancing bird health and improving livelihoods. Together, we can create disease-resilient poultry systems through knowledge, innovation, and timely action.
